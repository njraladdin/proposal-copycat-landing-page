Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Proposal Copycat ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Our extension collects the following information from your Upwork account:

  • Your archived proposals, including job titles and cover letters
  • Job descriptions from proposals you've submitted
  • Portfolio information that you manually input into the extension

Data Storage

All collected information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store any of your data on external servers. Your data remains entirely within your browser and under your control.

Data Usage

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Displaying your proposal history within the extension
  • Generating prompts to help write new proposals in your style
  • Improving your proposal writing process

Data Access

The extension requires access to:

  • upwork.com domain to collect proposal data
  • Chrome storage API to save your data locally
  • Active tab permissions to interact with Upwork's website

Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Clear all stored data at any time using the extension's interface
  • Choose how many historical proposals to include in prompt generation
  • Modify or delete your portfolio information

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. All functionality is self-contained within your browser.

Data Security

We implement security best practices to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No external network requests are made with your data
  • The extension operates entirely within your browser's security sandbox

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the extension's documentation.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact form.
